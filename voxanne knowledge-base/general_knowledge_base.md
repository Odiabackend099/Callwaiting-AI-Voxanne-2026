# Voxanne AI: General Knowledge Base

## Overview
Voxanne AI is an AI-powered call management platform designed for medical clinics and service businesses. Our platform handles inbound/outbound calls, appointment booking, and patient engagement using natural language AI agents.

## Core Features
- **AI Call Handling**: Natural conversation with patients
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Seamless calendar integration
- **Call Transfers**: Warm handoffs to human staff
- **Knowledge Base Integration**: RAG-powered information retrieval
- **Multi-channel Support**: Voice, SMS, and email
