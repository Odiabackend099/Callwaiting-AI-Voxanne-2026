# Voxanne AI: FAQ Knowledge Base

## Medical Compliance & Security

### Is Call Waiting AI HIPAA compliant?
Yes, Call Waiting AI is built with enterprise-grade security and is fully HIPAA compliant. We sign a BAA (Business Associate Agreement) with every medical practice.

### Does she store patient data?
Call Waiting AI only temporarily processes data needed for booking (name, reason for visit). All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, adhering to strict privacy standards.

### Can she handle medical emergencies?
Call Waiting AI is trained to recognize emergency keywords (e.g., 'bleeding', 'pain', 'breathing'). She immediately routes these calls to your emergency line or instructs the patient to hang up and dial 911/999.

### What about liability if she makes a mistake?
Call Waiting AI operates within your strict guardrails. If she is unsure, she executes a 'Human Escalation Protocol' to transfer the call or take a message. All calls are recorded for your review and liability protection.

## Medical Terminology & Scope

### Does Call Waiting AI understand BBL, Mommy Makeover, etc.?
Yes. Call Waiting AI is pre-trained on 500+ cosmetic and medical procedures, from BBLs and Rhinoplasties to CoolSculpting and Mohs surgery.

### Can she quote pricing?
Only if you want her to. We can configure her to give specific ranges (e.g., '$8k-$12k for BBL') or to defer pricing to the in-person consultation.

### What if a patient asks about recovery time?
Call Waiting AI delivers your approved clinical answers. For example: 'Dr. Chen typically advises 2 weeks off work for a tummy tuck.'

### Can she handle insurance questions?
Call Waiting AI can answer basic network questions ('Do you take Blue Cross?') and route complex billing inquiries directly to your billing coordinator.
