# Voxanne AI - Best Practices Guide

## Call Handling

### What Voxanne Does Well
- Greets callers professionally
- Answers common questions about services and pricing
- Captures caller information (name, phone, email)
- Offers available appointment slots
- Books appointments in your calendar
- Handles calls 24/7 without fatigue

### What Requires Human Touch
- Complex medical questions
- Discussing specific treatment plans
- Handling complaints or concerns
- Building rapport for nervous patients
- Discussing financing options in detail

### Handoff Protocol
When a caller needs human assistance:
1. Voxanne acknowledges the request
2. Explains a staff member will call back shortly
3. Transfers to available staff or voicemail
4. Logs the interaction for follow-up

## Optimization Tips

### 1. Keep Information Updated
- Update pricing if it changes
- Add new services to the knowledge base
- Update staff availability
- Refresh promotions seasonally

### 2. Monitor Call Quality
- Review call recordings weekly
- Check for patterns in transfers
- Identify common questions
- Update KB with new FAQs

### 3. Train Your Team
- Brief staff on Voxanne's capabilities
- Establish handoff procedures
- Create response templates
- Hold monthly review meetings

### 4. Leverage Analytics
- Track call volume trends
- Monitor booking conversion rates
- Identify peak call times
- Measure ROI monthly

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: New Patient Inquiry
**Caller**: "What services do you offer?"

**Voxanne Response**:
"We offer a range of aesthetic treatments including:
- Botox and dermal fillers
- Chemical peels and microneedling
- Laser skin resurfacing
- Lip enhancement

Would you like to book a consultation to discuss which treatment is right for you?"

### Scenario 2: Pricing Question
**Caller**: "How much does Botox cost?"

**Voxanne Response**:
"Botox treatments start at £150 per area. The cost depends on the number of areas treated. Most patients invest £200-400 for a full treatment. Would you like to book a consultation to discuss pricing for your specific goals?"

### Scenario 3: Availability Question
**Caller**: "When can I come in?"

**Voxanne Response**:
"We have availability this week:
- Tuesday at 2:00 PM
- Wednesday at 10:00 AM
- Thursday at 3:30 PM
- Friday at 11:00 AM

Which time works best for you?"

### Scenario 4: Complex Question
**Caller**: "I'm pregnant, can I still get Botox?"

**Voxanne Response**:
"That's a great question that requires a medical discussion with our practitioner. I'll have one of our team members call you back within 2 hours to discuss your specific situation. Can I get your contact information?"

## Troubleshooting

### Calls Not Being Booked
- Check calendar integration
- Verify staff availability is current
- Review Voxanne's responses
- Ensure clear booking instructions

### High Transfer Rate
- Add more FAQs to KB
- Train Voxanne on common questions
- Review call recordings
- Improve response templates

### Low Call Volume
- Promote phone number
- Check voicemail setup
- Verify call forwarding
- Review marketing channels
