- Average Botox/Filler consultation: £500-£1,500

For a typical clinic receiving 20 calls/day:
- 62% answer rate = 12 calls answered, 8 calls missed
- If 2 of those missed calls are surgical procedures: £19,000 lost per week
- Annual revenue loss: £988,000

THE ROOT CAUSES

1. Peak Hour Overwhelm
   - Lunch time: 12-2 PM (staff eating, patients checking in)
   - End of day: 4-6 PM (consultations running late)
   - Mondays: Everyone calling after weekend
   - Result: Multiple calls come in, receptionist can only handle one

2. Limited Hours
   - Most clinics: 9 AM - 5 PM, Monday-Friday
   - Patients call: 7 AM before work, 6 PM after work, weekends
   - Result: 40% of potential calls go unanswered

3. Staff Limitations
   - Receptionists need breaks, lunch, time off
   - Turnover requires constant retraining
   - Sick days mean no coverage
   - Vacation = lost calls
   - Result: Inconsistent answer rates

4. No Lead Qualification
   - Every call requires staff time
   - Price shoppers waste 10+ minutes
   - Non-qualified leads clog the schedule
   - Result: Wasted staff time, poor conversion

THE COMPETITIVE DISADVANTAGE

When a patient calls 3 clinics:
- Clinic A: Answers immediately (Voxanne)
- Clinic B: Goes to voicemail
- Clinic C: Answers after 30 seconds

Patient behavior:
- 95% book with whoever answers first
- 4% try the second clinic
- 1% try the third clinic

Result: Clinic A captures the patient. Clinics B & C lose.
