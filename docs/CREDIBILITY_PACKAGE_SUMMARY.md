## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Customers unwilling to participate in case studies
**Mitigation:** Offer incentives, start with satisfied customers, make process easy

### Risk: Press outreach gets no response
**Mitigation:** Personalize pitches, follow up, pitch multiple angles, consider PR agency

### Risk: Website updates take longer than expected
**Mitigation:** Use templates, prioritize critical pages, have backup plan

### Risk: Sales team doesn't adopt new materials
**Mitigation:** Train thoroughly, make materials easy to use, tie to incentives
